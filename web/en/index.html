Our DNA

Our code of conduct:
Open Source, Craftsmanship, Agility

Open source is a way of code: using is only the first step.

We firmly believe that open sourcing non-business critical components and contributing to existing projects leads to better software. With Open Source code must come readability, test, documentation and, in a broader sense, uncompromised quality. It is no surprise that the most important components (from the OS to service libraries) of most applications are Open Source components: Open Source ensures quality. We maintain and contribute to Open Source because it has a philosophical value (we share knowledge and contribute to the state of the art), but also because it has a business value: it makes for more robust software.

A craftsman is proud of his craft, just like we are proud of our production.

With outstanding software must come smart and scalable architectures, light components and testable business units. We use state of the art technology and architectures to ensure that the problems of the past stay in the past. We make sure that quality insurance, business scalability and responsibility modularization is always part of the project from the ground up. Each of our production is build to scale and to evolve.

More than a service provider, we are a partner in the success of your endeavor.

Being agile means that more than a contract, we share your vision and your goals. Our mission is to put our resources at the service of your business. A change in the specs? Let's talk about it! A pivot in the model? We can support your new ideas! We engage with human intelligence, not set in stone specifications.

Kotlin

Kotlin is a technology built by the industry, for the industry, and proofed by the industry.

Today, Kotlin is cutting edge, but no longer a risk:
Kotlin is massively used in major projects all around the world.

Kotlin offers many advantages:

  • Stability

    By enforcing best practices, projects experience less bugs.

  • Productivity

    By alleviating common problems, development is faster.

  • Portability

    Business code is only written & tested once, which ensures business conformity on every platforms.

  • Maintainability

    Being designed for readability, code bases are easier to evolve.

We use the Kotlin technology ecosystem to create
a technology that is slick and resistant.

Kotlin multi-platform:
a huge productivity boost!

For business code units, using Kotlin unlocks an amazing potential: the ability to truly develop once and deploy everywhere.
In addition to JVM and Android bytecode, Kotlin can compile to Javascript as well as native iOS assembly.

Kodein is at the root of the #1 multi-platform framework that allows a developer to create business code units that can run on Android, iOS, the Web, server and desktop software.

By creating and maintaining this framework, we can create business code that is 100% tested and iso between platforms, ensuring a level of code quality and confidence that is rarely achieved in the industry.
